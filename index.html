About KARE ACM STUDENT CHAPTER

Established as an international chapter, the KARE ACM Student Chapter has been a vibrant community since its inception. With over 1000 dedicated members, we stand as the largest chapter within our university. Our primary mission is to cultivate an enduring passion for computer science among students, fostering both personal and professional growth. We achieve this by organizing a wide array of events and activities, frequently enhancing and developing skills in our members.

Additionally, our chapter opens doors to extensive networking opportunities, allowing students to connect with experts across various fields, gaining valuable insights and guidance. At KARE ACM Student Chapter, we are not just a student chapter; we are a close-knit family of aspiring individuals, dedicated to stimulating the advancement of computer science and nurturing the potential within every member.
